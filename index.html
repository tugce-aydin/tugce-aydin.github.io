Terminology

A mini technical glossary for German, English, and Turkish materials terminology.

Gefüge Microstructure · Mikroyapı

The internal structure of a material, including phases, grains, and defects.

Streckgrenze Yield strength · Akma dayanımı

The stress level where permanent plastic deformation begins.

Röntgendiffraktometrie XRD · X-ışını kırınımı

A characterization method used to identify crystal structure and phases.

Korrosion Corrosion · Korozyon

Material degradation caused by chemical or electrochemical reactions.

Wärmebehandlung Heat treatment · Isıl işlem

Controlled heating and cooling to change material properties.

Bruchzähigkeit Fracture toughness · Kırılma tokluğu

Resistance of a material to crack propagation.

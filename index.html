About

I am a third-year Materials Science and Engineering student, currently building expertise at the interface of classical materials characterisation and modern data-driven methods. My technical foundation spans XRD analysis, ceramics, thermal barrier coatings, and corrosion science.

Outside the lab, I write about materials technology in the context of German industry — particularly sustainability transitions in steel, polymer chemistry, and critical raw material dependencies. I believe the next generation of materials engineers will need to be as comfortable with a dataset as with a diffractometer.

I am seeking internship opportunities in R&D and digital materials engineering roles, preferably in the DACH region.
